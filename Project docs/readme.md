# MIRAGE

## Overview

MIRAGE is an open and community-driven deception security initiative focused on creating uncertainty for adversaries across modern attack surfaces.

Traditional security solutions attempt to identify malicious activity after it occurs through signatures, behavioral analytics, anomaly detection, and historical indicators.

MIRAGE takes a different approach.

Instead of observing attacker behavior alone, MIRAGE manipulates attacker perception by introducing deceptive assets, attack paths, credentials, identities, services, and environments that appear legitimate but are designed to expose malicious intent.

The philosophy behind MIRAGE is simple:

Attackers depend on certainty.
MIRAGE introduces uncertainty.

By influencing the attacker's understanding of an environment, MIRAGE transforms reconnaissance, credential abuse, privilege escalation, lateral movement, and automation into opportunities for detection, disruption, and intelligence collection.

## Vision

The future of security is not merely detecting attackers.

The future of security is controlling what attackers believe.

MIRAGE aims to establish deception as a foundational security layer across cloud, identity, network, data, and AI ecosystems.

## Core Concepts

### Deception Assets

Resources intentionally designed to attract unauthorized interaction.

Examples include:

* Decoy IAM Roles
* Honey Credentials
* Decoy Service Accounts
* Fake Databases
* Decoy APIs
* Honeytokens
* False Network Services
* AI and Agent Decoys

### Deception Paths

Simulated attack routes designed to mirror realistic adversary objectives and movement patterns.

### Perceived Environment

A controlled reality presented to attackers that appears authentic but is intentionally engineered to reveal malicious behavior.

### Threat Intelligence Through Interaction

Every deception interaction becomes an intelligence source that can reveal attacker objectives, techniques, infrastructure, and intent.
